üéØ Healthcare-Specific Solutions

Reducing Bias in Medical AI

  • Include diverse patient demographics in training
  • Use domain knowledge to select relevant features
  • Employ ensemble methods combining multiple approaches
  • Regular validation with clinical experts
  • Multi-site validation studies
  • Address algorithmic fairness across populations

Reducing Variance in Medical AI

  • Collect larger, more representative datasets
  • Use cross-validation with multiple hospitals
  • Apply appropriate regularization techniques
  • Implement federated learning across institutions
  • Use data augmentation responsibly
  • Monitor performance across different sites
